About Me

Hi! My name is Fraser Dumayne and this is my very own website which is the result of me teaching myself HTML/CSS over the course of a couple of weeks. It is still a work in progress as I continue to learn new techniques in HTML/CSS.

I recently graduated from Edinburgh Napier University with a first degree in Cyber Security & Forensics and am now working in the cyber security industry.

Cyber Security

Since I was very young I’ve been interested in computers. This interest lead me to pursue a career in cyber security, officially starting with my studies at Edinburgh Napier University (BEng Cyber Security & Forensics) which gave me the ability to land a role as a Security Engineer. In the future I hope to achieve a role in penetration testing as this field significantly interests me. I am however also interested in purple teaming and big data.

Travel

Since having more freedom (and an income) as an adult, I have loved to spend that time travelling and exploring new cultures. I’m always on the lookout for my next adventure! I feel like travel is one of the best ways to spend your time as you get to experience new cultures and gain valuable memories. Some of my favourite trips were visiting China for a university funded trip and also studying in Melbourne as part of a semester abroad.

Photography

Photography is something that I have always enjoyed on a basic level but is something that I'm attempting to gain more insight into. You can see some of my favourite pictures in my gallery. This hobby accompanies my interest in travel as I can take pictures in exotic locations when I’m on the move.

Music

Hardly an interesting hobby (I mean who doesn’t like music) but something that I am passionate about as it accompanies most of my activities. I love going to gigs (particularly Rock or Punk shows) and I try to travel abroad to discover a new music festival when I can.

Film

Since being in high school and having a lot of film loving friends, I developed a great interest in films. Going to the cinema is such a great experience and a way to take my mind off of life for a few moments. I keep track of most of my film watching on Letterboxd if you fancy seeing what I’ve been watching.

Sports & Fitness

More recently (perhaps due to sitting down all day at university and work) is going to the gym. I really enjoy building fitness programmes for myself and seeing the changes physically. Maybe one day I’ll look like Chris Hemsworth… I also recently got into bouldering/climbing which I hope to get into much more in the future.
